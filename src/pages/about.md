I'm a software engineer with solid experience in building and evolving software systems using modern development practices and agile methodologies.

I enjoy analyzing technical feasibility, shaping solutions, and helping teams move from ideas to reliable production systems. My work focuses on mobile development with Kotlin, designing REST and GraphQL APIs, and applying principles such as TDD, Clean Code, and SOLID to create maintainable and scalable software.

This blog is where I share lessons learned, technical notes, and reflections on software architecture, engineering practices, and the process of building real-world systems.
